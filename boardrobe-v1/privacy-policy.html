Boardrobe

Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026. This Privacy Policy explains how Boardrobe collects, uses, and handles information when you use the Boardrobe Chrome extension and related services.

What Boardrobe does

Boardrobe helps users turn inspiration images into shoppable product matches. Users can manually upload images, and may also choose to connect a Pinterest account to import boards or Pins into Boardrobe.

Information Boardrobe may collect

Depending on how you use Boardrobe, we may collect:

  • Images you manually upload into the extension.
  • Shopping page product information visible on the page you scan, such as product names, prices, image URLs, and product links.
  • Pinterest account data you choose to connect, such as your Pinterest profile, boards, and Pins that you choose to import.
  • Technical and usage data needed to operate the service, such as error logs or request timing information.

How Boardrobe uses information

  • To generate visual product matches based on your selected inspiration images.
  • To let you browse, select, and import Pinterest boards or Pins when you choose to connect Pinterest.
  • To improve matching quality, troubleshoot bugs, and keep the service working properly.

Pinterest data

If you connect Pinterest, Boardrobe will access only the Pinterest data needed to provide the import experience, such as your profile, boards, and Pins. Boardrobe uses that data solely to let you select and import inspiration content into the extension.

Data sharing

Boardrobe does not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by service providers that help operate the product, such as hosting or infrastructure providers, only as needed to run Boardrobe.

Data retention

Boardrobe retains data only as long as reasonably necessary to operate the product, provide requested features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce policies.

Your choices

  • You can choose not to connect Pinterest.
  • You can remove imported or uploaded images from your Boardrobe session.
  • You can stop using the extension at any time.

Security

Boardrobe takes reasonable steps to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children's privacy

Boardrobe is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

Boardrobe may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at the same public URL with a revised effective date.
